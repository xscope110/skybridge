Instructions


One


Fully charge drone batterys and install the app WhatsApp on your smart phone.


Two


Attempt to send a message/picture on whatsapp (mobile data must be on). If reception can not be reached this is were you use the system.


Three


Insert phone into the phone holder whith whatsapp open and attatch it to the drone.


Four


Power on the drone and controller. Lift the contoller throttle stick to max and the back to min, this will arm the drone.


Five


Use both sticks to navigate your drone to a place that is void of obstacles and is above any ridge or mountain. Do not fly more than 150m away. Hover in the desired place for a minute.


Six


Bring drone back to launch area. Do not completely cut the throttle stick during descent.


Seven


Remove your phone and check your phone, the text/pic should of been sent. Repeat process in another location if failed. Pack up system and recharge batteries.
